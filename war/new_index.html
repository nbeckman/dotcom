I'm Nels Beckman, a nerd and professional programmer with a PhD. You can check my nerd credentials by visiting my software page which talks about some of my smaller, personal projects, or you can jump directly to my GitHub page. Most of the software I get paid to write is stamped "PROPRIETARY/CONFIDENTIAL" so you can't take a look at it. :-(

I got my PhD at Carnegie Mellon University in glorious Pittsburgh, Penna. USA, under the tutelage of Jonathan Aldrich. The program was in Software Engineering at the Institute for Software Research. If you have the background, and are suitably motivated, you might consider reading my thesis.

In the blog-o-sphere you can find me writing at Blogface.org, or if brevity is your thing, on Twitter at @nolacoaster. For private correspondence, try sending me an email.

Finally, I got all kinds of other weirdo stuff on here, like a list of every book I have read since March 2005 (when I started keeping track), a list of some computer problems I once had but fixed, or a page containing a few portmanteau words that I claim to have invented which is really pretty short but gets me traffic from Google so I keep it up there anyway.
